This is a compiled version of `cacheable-request@7.0.2`.

Suitable for folks that don't won't to use cacheable-request@10.x or later, or just can't use it because of esm requirement.

Note: only supported on node 12.x and higher.

To use it, add the following to your package.json:

  "cacheable-request": "https://github.com/seyoon20087/cacheable-request-compiled",


You may also use it in your `resolutions` or `overrides`
